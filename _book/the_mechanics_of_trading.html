Valuation

If you buy an asset that is under priced you will make money when it becomes correctly priced. There are two problems with this: arriving at the correct price, and knowing when the market price will revert to it. The assumption that the clearing price is not correct which is problematic. What is even more problematic, is that the demand and supply shedule will adjust itself in some finite timescale. Somehow.
